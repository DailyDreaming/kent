Track Description:

Haplotype blocks derived from common SNPs on Chromosome 21 by Perlegen as described in "Common High-Resolution Haplotypes." Patil, N. et. al. Science 293 (2001) [Science]. The location of each haplotype block is represented by a blue horizontal line with tall vertical blue bars at the first and last SNPs of the block. Individual SNPs are denoted by smaller black vertical bars. At multi-megabase resolution in dense mode clusters of tall blue bars may indicate hotspots for recombination. More information is available for the individual block selected at the "Outside Link" above and general information on the blocks at Perlegen's main haplotype map page http://www.perlegen.com/haplotype/
