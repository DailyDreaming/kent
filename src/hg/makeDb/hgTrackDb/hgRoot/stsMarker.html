This track shows locations of STSs (Sequence Tagged Sites) along the draft assembly. Many of these STSs have been mapped with genetic techiques (in the Genethon and Marshfield maps) or using radiation hybrid (the Stanford, and GeneMap99 maps) and YAC mapping (the Whitehead map) techniques. This track also shows the approximate position of FISH mapped clones. Many thanks to the researchers who worked on these maps, and to Greg Schuler, Arek Kasprzyk, Wonhee Jang, Terry Furey and Sanja Rogic for helping process the data.
