Description

Fgenesh++ predictions are based on Softberry's gene finding software.

Methods

Fgenesh++ uses both HMMs and protein similarity to find genes in a completely automated manner. For more information, see the paper
Solovyev V.V. (2001) "Statistical approaches in Eukaryotic gene prediction." In   Handbook of Statistical Genetics (eds. Balding D. et al.), John Wiley & Sons, Ltd., p. 83-127.)
