Description

Mononucleosome control track

The control data was produced from the lightly Micrococcal nuclease-digested paired-end sequenced genomic DNA fragments in the size range of 400-900 bps. Each feature corresponds to one end of the paired-end fragment with thick portion representing the sequenced 25 bps of that end. Features span 147 bps (displayed equivalently to mononucleosome data). Mapped reads were collapsed and color-coded from the lightest to the darkest in five shades according to the following category-based scale: 1 read instance, 2 inctances, 3-5 instances, 6-10 instances, more than 10 instances. Upon mousing over a feature, the number of read instances starting at that bp can be observed.

Mononucleosome sense strand reads

Mononucleosomal fragments were obtained by Micrococcal nuclease digestion of C. elegans lysates, DNA extraction, and sequencing of nucleosome-bound DNA fragments. Features represent inferred 147 bp cores from fragments that mapped to the sense strand of the reference genome. The thick portion of each feature (50bps) represents the sequenced part of the nucleosome-bound fragment. Sequences starting at the same bp were collapsed and displayed as shades of blue (from lightest to darkest) according to the following category-based scale:: 1 read instance, 2 read instances, 3-5 instances, 6-10 instances, more than 10 instances. Upon mousing over a feature, the number of read instances starting at that bp can be observed.

Mononucleosome antisense strand reads

Mononucleosomal fragments were obtained by Micrococcal nuclease digestion of C. elegans lysates, DNA extraction and sequencing of nucleosome-bound DNA fragments. Features represent inferred 147 bp cores from fragments that mapped to the antisense strands. The thick portion of each feature (50bps) represents the sequenced part of the core. Sequences starting at the same bp were collapsed and displayed as shades of green (from lightest to darkest) according to the following category-based scale: 1 read instance, 2 read instances, 3-5 instances, 6-10 instances, more than 10 instances.
