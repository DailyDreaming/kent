Description

WABA Alignment C. briggsae to C. elegans

Methods

The WABA alignment algorithm is described in Genome Research.

"Conservation, Regulation, Synteny, and Introns in a Large-scale C. briggsae-C. elegans Genomic Alignment" in Genome Research August, 2000; 10(8):1115-1125

Credits

The WABA alignment algorithm was developed by Jim Kent and Alan M. Zahler.

The C. briggsae genome data is version cb25.agp8 of 11 July 2002 (2002-07-11). The C. elegans genome data is version WS100 of 02 May 2003 (2003-05-02).
