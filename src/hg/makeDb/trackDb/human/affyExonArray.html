Methods

This track shows the genomic alignments of exons, probesets, probes, and gene probesets from the Affymetrix Exon array.

  • Probesets represent the regions interrogated by the probesets on the exon array. These are labeled in the format transcriptII_probesetID, where transcriptID represents the transcript cluster (roughy speaking: gene) and probesetID represents the probeset.
  • Exons represent exonic regions interrogated on the exon array, expected to be included or spliced out in their entirety. When design-time evidence suggested that some exon had alternative splice sites, the exon was subdivided into two or more exons. The set of exons for a single transcript cluster are displayed together as one track entry, and identified by the transcript cluster ID.
  • Probes represent the oligonucleotide probes tiled on the exon array
  • Gene Probesets represent sets of probesets that each interrogate the same trascript cluster, grouped into a single track entry and identified by the transcript cluster ID.
