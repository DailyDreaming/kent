Description

This track identifies alternative conserved (human-mouse) exons, as predicted by ACEScan.

Methods

Putative alternative conserved exons on mRNAs were identified using a machine-learning algorithm, regularized least-squares classification. Fur further details of the method used to generate this annotation, please refer to Yeo et al (2005).
