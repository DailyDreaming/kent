Description

This track shows DNase hypersensitive areas assayed in a large collection of cell types. Regulatory regions in general, and promoters in particular, tend to be DNase sensitive. Additional views of this dataset and additional documentation on the methods used for this track are available at the Uniform DNaseI HS page. The uniform peaks in that track are the basis for the clusters shown here, which combine data from the peaks of the different cell lines in that page.

Display conventions

A gray box indicates the extent of the hypersensitive region. The darkness is proportional to the maximum signal strength observed in any cell line. The number to the left of the box shows how many cell lines are hypersensitive in the region.

Credits

This track shows data from the UW and Duke ENCODE groups, with uniform processing by the ENCODE Analysis Working Group. The clustering was performed at UCSC.
