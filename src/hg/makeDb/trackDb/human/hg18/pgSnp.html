Description

This track displays variant base calls from personal genomes that have been made publicly available.

Display Conventions and Configuration

The alleles in the the details page are what is found on the plus strand. The nucleotide bases shown in the browser adjusts to the requested view, complementing and or reversing the nucleotides as needed.

The frequency shown by box color, mouse overs, and on the details page is the read frequency of the alleles.

Methods

Craig Venter (JCVI) (Levy et al.)
An overview is given here. This subtrack contains Venter's SNPs from the file HuRef.InternalHuRef-NCBI.gff. This was filtered for method 1 SNPs only, and any SNPs that had N as an allele were also filtered out.

James Watson (CSHL) (Wheeler et al.)
These SNPs came from the file: watson_snp.gff.gz under the bulk JW polymorphisms.
http://jimwatsonsequence.cshl.edu/cgi-perl/gbrowse/jwsequence/

Yoruban NA18507 (Illumina Cambridge/Solexa) (Bentley et al.)
HapMap individual NA18507, SNP calls by PSU

YH (YanHuang Project) (Wang et al.)
These SNPs are from the YanHuang Project, for a Han Chinese individual. The data is available from the YH database.

CEU trio NA12878, NA12891, NA12892; YRI daughter NA19240 (1000 Genomes)
