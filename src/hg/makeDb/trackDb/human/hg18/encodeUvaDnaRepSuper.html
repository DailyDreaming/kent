Description

This super track groups related tracks from University of Virginia DNA replication analysis. These tracks contain temporal profiling of DNA replication and origin of DNA replication in multiple cell lines.

1) UVa DNA Rep
This track corresponds to five different time points relative to the start of the DNA synthesis phase (S-phase) of the cell cycle.

2) UVa DNA Rep Seg
This track corresponds to replication timing categories for DNA synthesis. Replication is segregated into early specific (Early), mid specific (Mid), late specific (Late), and non-specific (PanS). The first three categories correspond to regions that replicated in a time point-specific manner; the latter category encompasses regions that replicated in a temporally non-specific manner.

3) UVa DNA Rep Ori
This track shows replication origins identified using the nascent strand method (Ori-NS), the bubble trapping method (Ori-Bubble) and the TR50 local minima method (Ori-TR50). Tracks are available for HeLa cells (cervix carcinoma) for all methods and GM06990 cells (lymphoblastoid) for Ori-NS.

4) UVa DNA Rep TR50
This annotation shows smoothed replication timing for DNA synthesis as the time of 50% replication (TR50).
