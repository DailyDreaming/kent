Description

This track displays blastz alignments of the Feb. 2002 mouse draft assembly to the human genome.

Methods

Blastz uses 12 of 19 seeds and this matrix:
  A C G T
A  91-114 -31-123
C-114 100-125 -31
G -31-125 100-114
T-123 -31-114 91

O = 400, E = 30, K = 3000, L = 3000, M = 50
A second pass is done at reduced stringency (7mer seeds and MSP threshold of K=2200) to attempt to fill in gaps (up to about 10Kbp). Lineage specific repeats are abridged during this alignment.
