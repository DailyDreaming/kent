Description

This track displays the agreement level between multiple-sequence alignments in ENCODE regions in the Sep 2005 ENCODE MSA freeze, which were generated by the programs MAVID, MLAGAN and TBA (v2). The agreement between every pair of alignments on a base by base resolution is displayed, as well as the overall mean agreement between all three alignment pairs.

Methods

The agreement for a given human base, is computed as the fraction of species other than human on which the two alignments exactly agree. Note that two alignments can agree by aligning the human to the same base in another species, or by both aligning the human base to a gap.

Note that when a human base is completely gapped by two programs there is perfect agreement. This is in a sense, a bit misleading, as the agreement in the alignment may simply be an artifact of missing sequence in the other species. So we also provide tracks showing the number of ungapped species for every base in every alignment (Align Gaps).
