Description

The consensus elements in this track were generated from the nine different combinations of three conservation algorithms (phastCons, binCons, and GERP) and three sequence alignment methods (TBA,MLAGAN and MAVID) applied to the ENCODE region sequences of 27 vertebrates as defined in the October 2005 ENCODE sequence freeze.

Three different stringencies were used. The loose set of constrained sequences represent bases identified as being constrained by any conservation algorithm on any alignment. The moderate set of constrained sequences is derived from bases shown to be constrained by at least two of the three conservation algorithms, on at least two of the three alignments. And finally the strict set of constrained sequences represent only those bases that were constrained using all three conservation programs on all three multi-sequence alignments.
