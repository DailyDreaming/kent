Description

MicroRNA regulatory targets predicted by TargetScanS. This track displays conserved mammalian microRNA target sites in the 3'-UTR regions of Refseq Genes.

Method

Putative miRNA binding sites on mRNAs were identified using 6-nucleotide seed sequences from all known miRNAs conserved among human, mouse, rat, dog and chicken. These seed sequences were probed against the 3'-end of conserved mRNAs from the five genomes. For further details of the methods used to generate this annotation, see Lewis, B.P. et al. (2005) in the References section below.

Credit

Thanks to Benjamin Lewis at MIT and the Whitehead Institute for providing this annotation. Additional information on microRNA target prediction is available at http://genes.mit.edu/targetscan.

References
