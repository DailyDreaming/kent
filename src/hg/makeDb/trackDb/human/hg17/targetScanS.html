Description

MicroRNA regulatory targets predicted by TargetscanS (hg17). This track displays conserved mammalian microRNA target sites in the 3'UTR regions of Refseq Genes.

Method

For a description of the methods used to generate this annotation, see Lewis, B.P. et al. (2005) in the References section below.

Credit

Thanks to Benjamin Lewis at MIT and the Whitehead Institute for providing this annotation. Additional information on microRNA target prediction is available at http://genes.mit.edu/targetscan.

References

Lewis, B.P. et al..Conserved Seed Pairing, Often Flanked by Adenosines, Indicates that Thousands of Human Genes are MicroRNA Targets. Cell 120(1), 15-20 (2005).
