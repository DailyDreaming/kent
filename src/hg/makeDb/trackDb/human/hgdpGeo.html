Description

This track shows the 657,000 SNPs genotyped in 53 populations worldwide by the Human Genome Diversity Project (HGDP). This track and several others are available from the HGDP Selection Browser.

Methods

Samples collected by the HGDP were genotyped for 657,000 SNPs at Stanford, using the Illumina BeadStation technology. The observed alleles were determined to be ancestral or derived by ???.
