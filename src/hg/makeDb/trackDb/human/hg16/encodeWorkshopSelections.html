Description

These tracks are the union of the eleven individual subtracks from each of the specified regions, Intergenic or Intronic, Distal or Proximal.

Intergenic Distal

This track is the collection of all the TransFrags/TARS from the Yale and Affy transfrags tracks, selected to be outside the transcript coding region of the Gencode genes and at least 5000 base pairs distant from any exon coding region of the Gencode genes. Thus, these are the TransFrags/TARS in the Intergenic ENCODE areas.

Intergenic Proximal

This track is the collection of all the TransFrags/TARS from the Yale and Affy transfrags tracks, selected to be outside the transcript coding region of the Gencode genes, and within 5000 bases of the exon coding regions of the Gencode genes. Thus, this set are the TransFrags/TARS in the Intergenic ENCODE regions, and nearby to the Gencode genes.

Intronic Distal

This track is the collection of all the TransFrags/TARS from the Yale and Affy transfrags tracks, that are within the transcript region of Gencode genes, but at least 5000 base pairs distant from any exon coding region of the Gencode genes. Thus, these are the TransFrags/TARS within the Gencode introns.

Intronic Proximal

This track is the collection of all the TransFrags/TARS from the Yale and Affy transfrags tracks, selected to be within the transcript coding region of the Gencode genes, and within 5000 bases of the exon coding regions of the Gencode genes. Thus, this set are the TransFrags/TARS within the introns of the Gencode genes and nearby to the exons.

Methods

This data was collected directly from the UCSC Genome browser via the Table Browser.
