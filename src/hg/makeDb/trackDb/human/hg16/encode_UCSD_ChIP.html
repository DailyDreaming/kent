Description

Genome-wide location analyses of TAF1 binding and RNA polymerase II binding were conducted using chromatin extracted from IMR90 (lung fibroblast), HCT116 (colon epithelial carcinoma), HeLa (cervix epithelial adenocarcinoma), and THP1 (blood monocyte leukemia) cells.

Methods

A PCR tiling array composed of 24537 non-repetitive sequences within the 44 ENCODE regions was manufactured by the Ren Lab at UC San Diego.

For each marker, there are three replicates. Each experiment is normalized using their median values; the P-value and R-value are calculated using the modified single array error model (Li Z, et al. (2003)). The P-value and R-value are then derived from the weighted average results of the replicates,

The scale is 0-16, corresponding to -log base 10.

Verification

There are three replicates for each of the eight experiments.

The array platform , the raw data and normalized data for each experiment, and the image files have all been deposited at the NCBI GEO Microarray Database.
