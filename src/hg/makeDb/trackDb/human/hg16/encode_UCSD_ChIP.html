A PCR tiling array composed of 24537 non-repetitive sequences within the the 44 ENCODE regions was manufactured by the Ren lab at UCSD. Genome-wide location analyses of TAF1 binding and RNA polymerase II binding were conducted using chromatin extracted from IMR90, HCT116, HeLa and THP1 cells.

There are three replicates for each of the eight experiments. The P-value were generated using the weighted average (methods described in Li Z et al. (2003) PNAS, 100:8184-8169) results of the corresponding three replicates. The scale is 0-16, corresponding to -log base 10.

The array platform , the raw data and normalized data for each experiment, and the image files have all been deposited at the NCBI GEO Microarray Database.
