DESCRIPTION

Genome-wide location analysis of histone H3 trimethyl lysine 4 (H3K4me3) was conducted employing ChIP/chip using chromatin extracted from GM06990 (lymphoblastoid) cells.

Cell line: GM06990K
Antibody: ab8580
Epitope: H3K4me3
DNA microarray: ENCODE2.1.1

METHODS

Chromatin from the cell line was cross-linked with 1% formaldehyde, precipitated with antibody binding to histone H3 trimethyl lysine 4, sheared and hybridized to a DNA array. DNA was not amplified prior to hybridization. The raw and transformed data files reflect fold enrichment over background, averaged over six replicates.

VERIFICATION

There are six replicates total: two technical replicates (chromatin preparations) for each of the three biological replicates (cell cultures). Raw and transformed (averaged) data can be downloaded from the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute ftp site.
