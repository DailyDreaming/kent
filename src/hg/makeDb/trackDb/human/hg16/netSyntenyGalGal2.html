Description

This track shows the chicken/$organism chains for syntenic regions of Chicken (Feb. 2004 - galGal2) and $Organism.

Methods

The Chicken/$Organism Alignment Net track (netGalGal2) was processed with netFilter -syn to produce this set of syntenic alignments.
