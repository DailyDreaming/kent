Description

PolyA+ RNA from Retinoic Acid Stimulated HL-60 Cells after 0, 2, 8, and 32 hours were hybridized to Affymetrix ENCODE oligonucleotide tiling arrays which have 25mer probes tiled every 21 bp on average in the non-repeat ENCODE regions.

Methods

Transcribed regions were generated by applying a cutoff to the abundance estimate or 'signal' associated with a 5% false positive rate in negative bacterial controls tiled on the array and joining probes within 50bp of probes which pass the threshold. If the length of a strech of positive probes is less than 50bp, they are filtered out. An estimate of RNA abundance (signal) using a Wilcoxon Sign Rank Scan Statistic where the median of all pairwise average values of PM-MM is calculated for all probe pairs within a sliding window of 101bp of a given central probe using quantile normalizing replicate arrays whose median array intensity was scaled to 25.

Verification

Three independent biological replicates were generated; these were used to hybridize 2 arrays (2 technical replicates). Transcribed regions were generated from this signal track based on a 5% FP cutoff in negative bacterial controls. These sites were then ranked, individually in each of the replicates, based on their log transformed pvalues as computed from the respective replicates. All pairwise differences in these ranks were computed among biological replicates and replicates were accepted when the sum of rank differences was relatively low (there is currently no hard cutoff criteria).
