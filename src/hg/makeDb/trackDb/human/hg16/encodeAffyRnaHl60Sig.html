Description

PolyA+ RNA from Retinoic Acid Stimulated HL-60 Cells after 0, 2, 8, or 32 hours were hybridized to Affymetrix ENCODE oligonucleotide tiling arrays which have 25mer probes tiled every 21 bp on average in the non-repeat ENCODE regions.

Methods

An estimate of RNA abundance (signal) using a Wilcoxon Sign Rank Scan Statistic where the median of all pairwise average values of PM-MM is calculated for all probe pairs within a sliding window of 101bp of a given central probe using quantile normalizing replicate arrays whose median array intensity was scaled to 25.

Verification

Three independent biological replicates were generated; these were used to hybridize 2 arrays (2 technical replicates). Transcribed regions were generated from this signal track based on a 5% FP cutoff in negative bacterial controls. These sites were then ranked, individually in each of the replicates, based on their signal as computed from the respective replicates. All pairwise differences in these ranks were computed among biological replicates and replicates were accepted when the sum of rank differences was relatively low (there is currently no hard cutoff criteria).
