Description

This track shows the FWD (+) and REV (-) strand transcript map of intensity scores (estimating RNA abundance) for Human Placental Poly(A)+ RNA hybridized to the Yale MAS (Maskless Array Synthesizer) ENCODE oligonucleotide microarray, transcription mapping design #1. This array has 36-mer oligonucleotide probes approximately every 36 bp (i.e., end-to-end) covering all the non-repetitive DNA sequence of the ENCODE regions ENm001-ENm012. See NCBI GEO GPL2105 for details of this array design. This transcript map is a combined signal from 3 biological replicates, each with at least 2 technical replicates. Arrays were hybridized using the protocol used in Bertone et al (2004).

Methods

A score is assigned to each oligonucleotide probe position by combining two or more technical replicates and by using a sliding window approach. Within a sliding window of 160 bp (corresponding to 5 oligos), the hybridization intensities for all replicates of each oligonucleotide probe are compared to their respective array median score. Within the window and across all the replicates, the number of probes above and below their respective median are counted. Using the sign test, a one-sided p-value is then calculated and a score defined as score=-log(p-value) is assigned to the oligo in the center of the window. Three independent biological replicates were generated and each was hybridized to at least 2 different arrays (technical replicates).

Verification

Reasonable correlation coefficients between replicates are ensured. Additionally, transcribed regions (TARs/transfrags) are called and compared between technical and biological replicate to ensure significant overlap.
