Description

This track shows the location of known and predicted non-protein coding RNA genes and pseudogenes that fall within the encode regions. It contains all information in Sean Eddy's RNA gene track for these regions combined with computational predictions generated by Jakob Skou Pedersen's evoFold algorithm. In addition to the fields contained in the RNA gene track, this track also includes encode-related fields describing overlap with transcribed regions and repeats.

Feature types include:
tRNA: Transfer RNA (or pseudogene)
rRNA: Ribosomal RNA (or pseudogene)
scRNA: Small cytoplasmic RNA (or pseudogene)
snRNA: Small nuclear RNA (or pseudogene)
snoRNA: Small nucleolar RNA (or pseudogene)
miRNA: MicroRNA (or pseudogene)
misc_RNA: Miscellaneous other RNA, such as Xist (or pseudogene)

Color legend:
BLACK: Region is Repeatmasked.
GREEN: Region is transcribed.
RED: Region is from the RNA Gene track, and is not transcribed.
BLUE: Region is an evoFold prediction, and is not transcribed.

Methods

The RNA Gene track was supplemented with Jakob Skou Pedersen's evoFold predictions and filtered down to entries that lie within the Encode regions. Regions that are at least 10 percent repeatmasked are flagged because no transcriptional data is available for them. A region is considered transcribed if at least 10 percent overlaps with any Affymetrix transfrag (derived from 6 microarray experiments) or Yale TAR (derived from 15 microarray experiments.) Each array is listed from which the overlapped transfrags and TARs were derived.
