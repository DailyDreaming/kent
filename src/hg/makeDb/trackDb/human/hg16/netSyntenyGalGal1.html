Description

This track shows the chicken/$organism chains for syntenic regions of Chicken (Jan. 2004 - galGal1) and $Organism.

Methods

The Chicken/$Organism Alignment Net track (netHg16) was processed with netFilter -syn to produce this set of syntenic alignments.
