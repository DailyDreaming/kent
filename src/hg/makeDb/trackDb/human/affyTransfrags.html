Description.

This track represents regions (transfrags) of chromosomes 6, 7, 13, 14, 19, 20, 21, 22, X, and Y that are thought to be transcribed based on the transcriptome data from Affymetrix. This track is a preview of the phase two Affymetrix transcriptome project and is based on hybridzation of mRNA from the SK-N-AS cell line. Data from seven additional cell lines will be available when the project is completed.

Methods

mRNA was isolated and hybridized to tiling GeneChips that contain a probe ever 5bp over chromosomes 6, 7, 13, 14, 19, 20, 21, 22, X, and Y (over 74 million probes total). The resulting data was normalized and smoothed using the Hodges-Lehmann estimator associated with the Wilcoxon signed rank statistic of the pm - mm values that lie within +/- 30bp of a sliding window centered at every genomic coordinate. To determine the locations of the transfrags the following method was used:

  • A threshold of 13.93 (which gives an average FP rate of ~ 0.05 in negative bacterial controls)
  • was applied to all the signal graphs and defined coordinates as positive. Positive coordinates
  • separated by gaps less than or equal to 30bp were merged. Resulting regions that were less than
  • 50bp in length were filtered out. Finally, 12bp were added to all start coordinates and 13bp to
  • all stop coordinates (ie. interbase coordinates) which shifts the coordinates to be centered over
  • probes as opposed to being left justified with respect to probes as is the case in the
  • raw and graph data.
