Description

This track displays the trend p-values (-log10) of the seven diseases reported by the Case Control Consortium in their Nature paper. Those diseases are:

  • Bipolar disorder
  • Coronary artery disease
  • Crohn's disease
  • Hypertension
  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Type 1 diabetes
  • Type 2 diabetes

Methods

Reported trend p-values are taken for each of the Affy 500k probes on the genome, then logged and multiplied by -1 for visualization purposes. Most of chromosome X data is omitted because of the difference in statistical power and the difference in control sets used. The genotypic p-value is not used for this track, because the trend p-value captures the majority of the associated signals anyway.

For the complete dataset, visit The Wellcome Trust Case Control Consortium Web Site.
