Human RNA Editing

Currently only A-to-I and C-to-U editing instances are in the data. Most edits are Adenosine in chromosome becomes Inosine in RNA via de-amination. Rarely, Cytidine becomes Uridine via de-amination.

Inosine is read as guanosine during translation. A-to-I editing may alter codons, splice signals and RNA secondary structure.

RNA editing locations are displayed as vertical dashes, which are colour coded as follows:
5' UTR : Dark Green
CDS : Blue
Intron : Red
3' UTR : Deep Pink
Other (exon/intron status is unclear or unknown) : Black

The data were produced by University College Cork.
