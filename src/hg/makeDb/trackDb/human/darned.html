Description

Human RNA Editing

This track provides information on genomic coordinates corresponding to RNA nucleotides that are being edited after transcription. The track contains information on A-to-I and C-to-U RNA editing that occur due to deamination of adenosines and cytidines by ADAR and APOBEC enzymes, respectively. Most of the data are on A-to-I editing that is known to be highly abundant in humans.

Display

The coordinates corresponding to edited nucleotides are differentially colored depending on their occurrence within RNA transcripts:
5' UTR : Dark Green
CDS : Blue
Intron : Red
3' UTR : Deep Pink
Other (exon/intron status is unclear or unknown) : Black

Methods

The data were obtained from several research papers on RNA editing and were mapped to the reference genome. More detailed information (supporting EST, tissue sources, literature referenes, etc) can be obtained from DARNED database.

References:

Kiran, A, Baranov P.V. DARNED: a DAtabase of RNa EDiting in humans Bioinformatics 26(14):1772-6 (2010)
