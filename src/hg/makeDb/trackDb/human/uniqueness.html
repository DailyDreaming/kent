Description

This track shows unique regions in the genome, as identified by the genomic alignment of 35-nucleotide segments.

Methods

Stepping across the entire genome in 1-nucleotide steps, we generated sequence 'tiles' of 35 nucleotides in length. We aligned each tile to the genome using the BWA aligner. If a tile aligns uniquely and perfectly in hg18, BWA assigns it a p-value of 1e-37. If it aligns uniquely in multiple locations, BWA assigns it a p-value of 0. For each tile, we recorded the oligo mid-point coordinate and the -log_10 p-value: 37 (unambiguous) to 0 (ambiguous).
