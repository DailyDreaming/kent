Description

This track contains the annotations of chromosome 22 produced by the Chromosome 22 Group at the Sanger Institute. These annotations were obtained from the internet at http://www.sanger.ac.uk/HGP/Chr22. They are described in the paper Collins, J.E., Goward, M.E., Cole, C.G., Smink, L.J., Huckle, E.J., Knowles, S., Bye, J.M., Beare, D.M. & Dunham I. (2003) Reevaluating Human Gene Annotation: A Second Generation Analysis of Human Chromosome 22. Genome Res. 13(1):27-36.
