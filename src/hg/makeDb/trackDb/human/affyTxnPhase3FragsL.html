Description (change track name to “Affy Txn lRNA regions”)

This track displays transcriptome data from tiling GeneChips produced by Affymetrix. For the complete non-repetitive portion of the human genome, more than 256 million probe pairs were tiled every 5 bp in non-repeat-masked areas and hybridized to cytosolic polyA+ long RNA (>200 nucleotides) from 8 different cell lines (some cell lines were female and contain no data for chrY). For HeLa and HepG2, in addition to cytosolic polyA+ RNA, samples were also produced for nuclear RNA. For experimental details and results, see Kapranov et al. in the References section below. This track contains the coordinates of transcribed fragments (transfrags) representing long RNAs.

Methods

For each data point, probes within 30 bp on either side were used to improve the estimate of expression level for a particular probe. This helped to smooth the data and produce a more robust estimate of the transcription level at a particular genomic location. The following analysis method was used:

1.      Replicate arrays were quantile-normalized and the median intensity (using both PM and MM intensities) of each array was scaled to a target value of 50.

2.      The expression level was estimated for each mapped probe position by

o        collecting all the probe pairs that fell within a window of ± 30 bp

o        calculating all non-redundant pairwise averages of PM - MM values of all probe pairs in the window

o        taking the median of all resulting pairwise averages

3.      The resulting signal value is the Hodges-Lehmann estimator associated with the Wilcoxon signed-rank statistic of the PM - MM values that lie within ± 30 bp of the sliding window centered at every genomic coordinate. This data is displayed in the track “Affy Txn lRNA signals”.

4.      Transcribed regions (transfrags) were determined by connecting adjacent positive probes with a maximum allowable gap of 11 nucleotides and a minimum run of 49 nucleotides (at least 11 probes with at most 1 negative probe in-between 2 positive probes). Transfrags which overlap pseudogenes, or harboring low complexity or repetitive sequences were discarded.
