Description

This track shows per-continent integrated haplotype score (iHS, Voight et al.) calculated using SNPs genotyped in 53 populations worldwide by the Human Genome Diversity Project in collaboration with the Centre d'Etude du Polymorphisme Humain (HGDP-CEPH). This track and several others are available from the HGDP Selection Browser.

The score shown for each SNP is -log10 of the ??? SNP's position in the distribution of windowed iHS score ???.

Methods

Samples collected by the HGDP-CEPH were genotyped for 657,000 SNPs at Stanford, using the Illumina BeadStation technology. The 53 populations were divided into seven continental groups: Africa, Middle East, Europe, South Asia, East Asia, Oceania and the Americas. iHS was then calculated as described in (Voight et al.). ??? Per-SNP iHS scores were smoothed in windows of 31 SNPs, centered on each SNP. The final score is -log10 of the proportion of smoothed scores higher than each SNP's smoothed score. ???
