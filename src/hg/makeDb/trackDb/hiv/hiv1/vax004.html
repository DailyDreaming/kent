Description

The VAX004 Sequence track shows VAX004 HIV-1 gp120 nucleotide sequences aligned against the base genome.

Methods

VAX004 HIV-1 gp120 nucleotide sequences were aligned to the base genome using BLAT. The resulting alignments with minimum identity of 80% and BLAT score equal or better than 200 were used to construct this track.

Credits

This track was produced at UCSC.

References

Kent WJ. BLAT - the BLAST-like alignment tool. Genome Res. 2002 Apr;12(4):656-64.
