Description

This track shows Multi-Species Conserved Sequences (MCS) found by intersecting the conserved sequences generated using the binomial-based and parsimony p-value-based methods.

Methods

Further information about the methods used to produce these data is available in the paper Margulies, E. et al. (2003) "Identification and Characterization of Multi-Species Conserved Sequences". Genome Res. 13:2507-2518.
