Description

This track shows Multi-Species Conserved Sequences (MCS) found by intersecting the MCSs generated using the binomial-based and parsimony p-value-based methods. Regions less than 10 bp apart have been merged into one region.

Methods

Further information about the methods used to produce these data is available in the paper Margulies, E. et al. (2003) "Identification and Characterization of Multi-Species Conserved Sequences". Genome Res. 13:2507-2518.
