Description

This track shows Multi-Species Conserved Sequences (MCS) found by intersecting the MCSs generated using the binomial-based and parsimony p-value-based methods. Regions less than 10 bp apart have been merged into one region.

Methods

Further information about the methods used to produce these data will be available in the paper Margulies et al. "Multi-Species Sequencing of Targeted Genomic Regions: Comparative Studies of Genome Evolution and Function". Submitted to Genome Res.
