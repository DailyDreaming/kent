Description

This track depicts the conservation score resulting from an analysis of the 12 species represented in the blastz alignments.

Methods

Conservation scores were generated by weighing the relative contribution of each species sequence to the overall conservation score, based on how diverged each species was from the reference sequence. This weighting scheme was determined by calculating the neutral substitution rates at 4-fold degenerate positions.

Additional information about this conservation score will be available in the paper Margulies, E. et al. "Multi-Species Sequencing of Targeted Genomic Regions: Comparative Studies of Genome Evolution and Function". Submitted to Genome Res.
