Description

This track depicts Multi-Species Conserved Sequences (MCS) defined using a conservation score threshold from the parsimony p-value method that selects the top 5% most conserved sequence.

Methods

Additional information about the methods used to produce these data will be available in the paper Margulies, E. et al. "Multi-Species Sequencing of Targeted Genomic Regions: Comparative Studies of Genome Evolution and Function". Submitted to Genome Res.
