Description

This track displays NISC-generated, curated gene annotations for the human genome and each of the selected species. Coding exons are depicted taller than 5' and 3' untranslated regions (UTR). Connecting introns are one-pixel lines with hatch marks indicating direction of transcription.

Methods

Exon locations of known genes were determined by aligning human and/or mouse reference cDNA sequences with the assembled genomic sequence of each species. When splice site consensus, exon structure, or protein translation errors were encountered, they were manually inspected and corrected.
