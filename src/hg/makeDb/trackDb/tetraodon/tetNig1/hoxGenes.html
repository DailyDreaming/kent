Description

This track shows HOX gene annotations which were annotated by human experts using information on sequence similarity between Tetraodon and human, mouse and zebrafish HOX protein and nucleotide sequences. HOX Genes annotations are only on the following chromosomes: 2, 8, 9, 17, 21 and Un_random. On other chromsomes, the track control will not appear.
