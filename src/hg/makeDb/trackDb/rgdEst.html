Description

An Expressed Sequence Tag (EST) is a partial sequence of a randomly-chosen cDNA, obtained from the results of a single DNA sequencing reaction. ESTs are used to identify transcribed regions in genomic sequence and to characterize patterns of gene expression in the tissue from which the cDNA was derived.

Methods

The annotation data file, RGD_EST.gff, was downloaded from

       ftp://rgd.mcw.edu/pub/RGD_genome_annotations/V3.1/GFF_files/

and processed to create two UCSC Genome Browser tables -- rgdEst and rgdEstLink -- to enable this track.
