Description

The miRNA track shows microRNAs from the miRNA Registry at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute. Mature miRNAs (miR) are represented by a thick line, and the predicted stem-loop portions of the primary transcripts by a thinner line. miRNAs in the sense orientation are black, and those in the reverse orientation are grey. When a single precursor produces two mature miR from its 5' and 3' parts, it is presented twice, with the different positions of the mature miR.

Methods

Mature and precursor miRNA from the miRNA Registry release 3.0 are aligned against the genome using BLAT. The extents of the precursor sequences are not generally known, and are predicted based on base paired hairpin structure. The miRNA Registry is described in Griffiths-Jones S (2004), "The microRNA Registry", Nucl. Acids Res. 32:D109-D111.
