Description

This track displays EasyGene gene predictions done by the Krogh lab at the University of Copenhagen. The prediction highlighted in red corresponds to the genbank prediction. In the browser view, black features are CDS, dark gray features are ALT, and lighter gray features are ORF.

References

  • Larsen TS, Krogh A. EasyGene--a prokaryotic gene finder that ranks ORFs by statistical significance. BMC Bioinformatics. 2003 Jun 03;4(1):21. PubMed Abstract.
