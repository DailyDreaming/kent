Description

This track shows Simple Nucleotide Polymorphisms and/or insertions/deletions (SNPs) determined by the Beijing Genomics Institute (BGI). Genomic sequence reads from three alternate strains of chicken were used to determine the SNPs relative to the reference Chicken assembly (Red Jungle Fowl): Broiler, Layer and Silkie. When a SNP is associated with a gene annotated by BGI, information about the association is displayed along with a link to the details page for the gene.

Methods

Sequence reads from the Broiler, Layer and Silkie strains were mapped to the reference Chicken assembly with blast. Then cross_match was used for the detailed base-by-base comparison. The Phred quality scores (-10 * log_10 estimated error probability) are given for the SNP base(s) in the assembly and the SNP read.
