Description

This track shows areas of coverage for genomic regions of 3 alternate strains of chicken sequenced by the Beijing Genomics Institute (BGI): Broiler, Layer, and Silkie. Single reads from those three strains were mapped to the reference assembly for Red Jungle Fowl (RJF).

Coverage area names are of the format Strain.Chrom.Start.End:

  • Strain: The read was from Broiler, Layer or Silkie
  • Chrom: The read was mapped to this chromosome in the RJF reference assembly
  • Start: First base in Chrom of mapped read
  • End: Last base in Chrom of mapped read
