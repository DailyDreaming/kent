Description

This track shows exon-anchored synteny blocks for $Organism and Chimp (Nov. 2003 - panTro1), taken from a 5-species synteny map (chicken, human, chimp, mouse, rat). The map is 1-to-1 and symmetric, so segment IDs are common to all pairwise tracks generated from the map.
