Description

TWINSCAN predictions proposed for exploratory PCR confirmation.

Method

Multi-exon TWINSCAN predictions that

  1. do not overlap MGC genes or MGC in progress ESTs.
  2. fail to have any splice site supported by a human EST or mRNA, except for ESTs or mRNAs that also map to another prediction nearly as well.
  3. do not overlap MGC A-list
  4. are not inside any Genbank human mRNA.
inside means [begin, end] of the alignment of a genbank human mRNA contains the [begin, end] of a Twinscan prediction.
