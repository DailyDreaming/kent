Description

This track shows the position of gaps -- represented by Ns -- within the Ciona assembly. Gaps of 50 or more bases are likely to have been introduced by the JGI JAZZ assembler.

For a discussion of gaps and the JAZZ assembler see The Draft Genome of Ciona intestinalis: Insights into Chordate and Vertebrate Origins (Supplemental Materials). Science. 2002 Dec 13;298(5601):2157-67.
