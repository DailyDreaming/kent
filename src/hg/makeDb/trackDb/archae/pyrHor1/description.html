The Pyrococcus horikoshii OT3 genome sequence is 1,738,505 bp long and has approximately 1955 protein-coding genes (NCBI reviewed).  P. horikoshii is a hyperthermophilic archaeon isolated from a hydrothermal vent at a depth of 1395 meters in the Okinawa Trough in the Pacific Ocean. It grows optimally at 98 C.

The sequence was published in 1998 by Kawarabayasi et al. at the National Institute of Technology Evaluation (NIST) in Tokyo, Japan.Sample position queries

A genome position can be specified by chromosomal coordinate range, COG ID, or keywords from the GenBank or TIGR description of an gene. The following list shows examples of valid position queries for this genome. 

Request:
    Genome Browser Response:

chr
Displays all of chromosome (the entire genome).
chr:1-10000
Displays first ten thousand bases on the chromosome.
NADH
Lists genes with "NADH" in the description.
PH1011
Display chromosome at position of gene PH1011.
PH101
Lists genes with names beginning with "PH101" (i.e., PH1010, PH1011, PH1012, etc).
