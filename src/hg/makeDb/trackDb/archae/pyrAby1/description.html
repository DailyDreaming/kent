The Pyrococcus abyssi GE5 genome sequence is 1,765,118 bp long and contains approximately 1896 protein-coding genes, plus a 3,444 bp plasmid.  P. abyssi is a hyperthermophilic archaeon isolated from samples taken close to a hot spring situated 3500 meters deep in the south-east Pacific. The optimal growth conditions are 103 C at 200 atmospheres pressure.  

The sequence was release in July 1999 by Genoscope, a collaboration among four teams led by Daniel Prieur (CNRS, Roscoff, France), Patrick Forterre (Universiti d'Orsay, France), Jean-Claude Thierry (IGBMC, Strasbourg, France), and Jacques Dietrich (IFREMER, Brest,France). The original source for the sequence and re-annotation efforts by the authors are located at the Genoscope P. abyssi web site.

Sample position queries

A genome position can be specified by chromosomal coordinate range, COG ID, or keywords from the GenBank or TIGR description of an gene. The following list shows examples of valid position queries for this genome. 

Request:
    Genome Browser Response:

chr
Displays all of chromosome (the entire genome).
chr:1-10000
Displays first ten thousand bases on the chromosome.
NADH
Lists genes with "NADH" in the description.
PAB1011
Display chromosome at position of gene PAB1011.
PAB101
Lists genes with names beginning with "PAB101".
