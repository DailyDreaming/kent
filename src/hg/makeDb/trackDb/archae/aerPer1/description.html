The Aeropyrum pernix genome sequence is 1,669,695 bp long and contains approximately 2,694 ORFs.  A. pernix is an aerobic hyperthermophilic archaeon that grows optimally at around 95C.  The sequence was completed in Apr 1999 by the National Institute of Technology and Evaluation in Tokyo, and was published in Complete genome sequence of an aerobic hyper-thermophilic crenarchaeon, Aeropyrum pernix K1.  Kawarabayasi Y, Hino Y, Horikawa H, Yamazaki S, Haikawa Y, Jin-no K, Takahashi M, Sekine M, Baba S, Ankai A, Kosugi H, Hosoyama A, Fukui S, Nagai Y, Nishijima K, Nakazawa H, Takamiya M, Masuda S, Funahashi T, Tanaka T, Kudoh Y, Yamazaki J, Kushida N, Oguchi A, Kikuchi H, et al.  DNA Res. 1999, 6(2):83-101, 145-52.

Sample position queries

A genome position can be specified chromosomal coordinate range, COG ID, or keywords from the GenBank or TIGR description of an gene. The following list shows examples of valid position queries for the human genome. 

Request:
    Genome Browser Response:

chr
Displays all of chromosome (the entire genome).
chr:1-10000
Displays first ten thousand bases on the chromosome.
NADH
Lists genes with "NADH" in the description.
APE0772
Display chromosome at position of gene APE0772.
APE077
Lists genes with names beginning with "APE077".
