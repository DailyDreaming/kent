The current draft of the Methanosarcina barkeri str. fusaro genome is 4.87 Million bp long. M. barkeri is a mesophilic methanogen isolated from mud at a freshwater lake near Naples, Italy.

The most recent genome sequence draft was released June 2002 by the DOE Joint Genome Institute, but there is no associated genome publication to date.
