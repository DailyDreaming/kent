The Thermococcus kodakarensis KOD1 genome has 1 chromosome that is 2,088,737 bp long and contains approximately 2,306 predicted proteins.  T. kodakarensis is a hyperthermophile that grows optimally at 80-95C. It can metabolize a variety of compounds including sulfur. The sequences were completed in Jan 2005 by Kyoto University, and was published in Complete genome sequence of the hyperthermophilic archaeon Thermococcus kodakaraensis KOD1 and comparison with Pyrococcus genomes. .  Fukui T, Atomi H, Kanai T, Matsumi R, Fujiwara S, Imanaka T.
