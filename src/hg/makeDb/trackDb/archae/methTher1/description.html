The Methanothermobacter thermoautotrophicus genome has 1 chromosome that is 1,751,377 bp long and contains approximately 1,873 predicted proteins.  M. thermoautotrophicus is anaerobic and thermophilic. It grows at pH 7.2-7.6, and produces methane by reducing carbon dioxide. The sequence was completed in Nov 1997 by Oscient Pharmaceuticals Corporation, and was published in Complete genome sequence of Methanobacterium thermoautotrophicum deltaH: functional analysis and comparative genomics.  Smith DR, Doucette-Stamm LA, Deloughery C, Lee H, Dubois J, Aldredge T, Bashirzadeh R, Blakely D, Cook R, Gilbert K, Harrison D, Hoang L, Keagle P, Lumm W, Pothier B, Qiu D, Spadafora R, Vicaire R, Wang Y, Wierzbowski J, Gibson R, Jiwani N, Caruso A, Bush D, Reeve JN, et al.
