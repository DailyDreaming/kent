The Dec. 2001 Pyrobaculum aerophilum genome is a crenarchaea with a is 2.2-megabase long genome. It grows optimally at 100C and was found near boiling marine water holes in Italy. The organsim is capable of both microaerobic and anaerobic metabalism. The sequence is based on the one submitted to GenBank 12 Dec. 2001 in GenBank, by Sorel Fitz-Gibbon and Jeffrey Miller.
