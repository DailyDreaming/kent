The Sulfolobus solfataricus P2 genome sequence is 2,992,245 bp long and contains approximately 2,977 protein-coding genes, of which 40% are archaeal specific.  S. solfataricus is an acidophile growing optimally at a pH 2-4.  The sequence was completed in June 2001 by the University of Copenhagen, and was published in She et al. (2001) Proc Natl Acad Sci. USA 98(14):7835-40.

Sample position queries

A genome position can be specified by chromosomal coordinate range, COG ID, or keywords from the GenBank or TIGR description of an gene. The following list shows examples of valid position queries for this genome. 

Request:
    Genome Browser Response:

chr
Displays all of chromosome (the entire genome).
chr:1-10000
Displays first ten thousand bases on the chromosome.
NADH
Lists genes with "NADH" in the description.
SSO0570
Display chromosome at position of gene SSO0570.
SSO057
Lists genes with names beginning with "SSO057".
