The Pyrococcus furiosus DSM 3638 genome sequence is 1,908,256 bp long and contains approximately 2,000 protein-coding genes.  P. furiosus is a hyperthermophilic archaeon found near deep-sea volcanoes, where it optimally grows at around 100C.  The sequence was completed in Feb 2002 by the University of Utah, and was published in Genomic sequence of hyperthermophile, Pyrococcus furiosus: implications for physiology and enzymology.  Robb FT, Maeder DL, Brown JR, DiRuggiero J, Stump MD, Yeh RK, Weiss RB, Dunn DM.  Methods Enzymol. 2001;330:134-57.

Sample position queries

A genome position can be specified chromosomal coordinate range, COG ID, or keywords from the GenBank or TIGR description of an gene. The following list shows examples of valid position queries for the human genome. 

Request:
    Genome Browser Response:

chr
Displays all of chromosome (the entire genome).
chr:1-10000
Displays first ten thousand bases on the chromosome.
NADH
Lists genes with "NADH" in the description.
PF1011
Display chromosome at position of gene PF1011.
PF101
Lists genes with names beginning with "PF101".
