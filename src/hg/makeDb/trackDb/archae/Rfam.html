Description

This track shows current known or predicted non-coding RNA (ncRNA) genes from the Rfam database.

Credits

This track was produced at UCSC with data obtained from the Rfam database. Rfam is a joint project involving researchers based at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute, and Washington University, St. Louis. Rfam makes use of a large amount of available data, especially published multiple sequence alignments, and repackages these data in a single searchable and sustainable resource.
