The Sulfolobus tokodaii str.7 genome has 1 chromosome that is 2,694,756 bp long and contains approximately 2,825 predicted proteins.  S. tokodaii is a hyperthermophilic acidophile. It grows optimally at pH 2.5-3, and approximately 80C. S. tokodaii is an obligate aerobe and metabolizes sulfur. The sequence was completed in Sep 2001 by NITE, and was published in Complete genome sequence of an aerobic thermoacidophilic crenarchaeon, Sulfolobus tokodaii strain7 .  Kawarabayasi Y, Hino Y, Horikawa H, Jin-no K, Takahashi M, Sekine M, Baba S, Ankai A, Kosugi H, Hosoyama A, Fukui S, Nagai Y, Nishijima K, Otsuka R, Nakazawa H, Takamiya M, Kato Y, Yoshizawa T, Tanaka T, Kudoh Y, Yamazaki J, Kushida N, Oguchi A, Aoki K, Masuda S, Yanagii M, Nishimura M, Yamagishi A, Oshima T, Kikuchi H.
