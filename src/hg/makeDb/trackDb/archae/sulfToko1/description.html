The Sulfolobus tokodaii str. 7 genome is 2.69 Million bp long and contains approximately 2874 predicted genes. S. tokodaii is a hyperthermophilic acidophile. It grows optimally at pH 2.5-3 at approximately 80C. S. tokodaii is an obligate aerobe and metabolizes sulfur.

The sequence was released August 2001 by NITE, and was described in DNA Res 8:123-40 (2001) Kawarabayasi Y, Hino Y, Horikawa H, Jin-no K, Takahashi M, et al.  "Complete genome sequence of an aerobic thermoacidophilic crenarchaeon, Sulfolobus tokodaii strain7. "
Abstract: The complete genomic sequence of an aerobic thermoacidophilic crenarchaeon, Sulfolobus tokodaii strain7 which optimally grows at 80 degrees C, at low pH, and under aerobic conditions, has been determined by the whole genome shotgun method with slight modifications. The genomic size was 2,694,756 bp long and the G + C content was 32.8%. The following RNA-coding genes were identified: a single 16S-23S rRNA cluster, one 5S rRNA gene and 46 tRNA genes (including 24 intron-containing tRNA genes). The repetitive sequences identified were SR-type repetitive sequences, long dispersed-type repetitive sequences and Tn-like repetitive elements.
