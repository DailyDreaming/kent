The Ferroplasma acidarmanus Fer1 genome is 1.87 Million bp long and contains approximately 0 predicted genes. F. acidarmanus is an acidophilic iron-oxidizing euryarchaeon that is native to metal-rich environments (including those rich in arsenic). It grows optimally at 42C and pH 1.2. The genome is still in draft form.

The most recent genome sequence draft was released June 2005 Draft by US DOE Joint Genome Institute, but there is no associated genome publication to date.
