Description

This track shows non-protein-coding genes annotated by FlyBase and the Drosophila Heterochromatin Genome Project (DHGP). Annotations on the non-heterochromatin sequences (chr2L, chr2R, chr3L, chr3R, chr4, chrM, chrX) are FlyBase D. melanogaster version 5.1. Annotations on the heterochromatin sequences (chr2LHet, chr2RHet, chr3LHet, chr3RHet, chrXHet, chrYHet, chrU) were taken from the supplemental data of Smith et al. (see References below).
