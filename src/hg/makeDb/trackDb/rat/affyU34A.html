Description

This track shows the location of the exemplar sequences used for the selection of probes on the Affymetrix RG-U34A chip.

Methods

Exemplar sequences were downloaded from the Affymetrix Product Support and mapped to the genome using blat followed by pslReps with the parameters -minCover=0.3 -minAli=0.95 and -nearTop=0.005.
