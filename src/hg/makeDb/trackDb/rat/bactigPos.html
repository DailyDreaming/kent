Description

Instead of clone-by-clone assemblies, the Rat genome assembly is pieced together from Bactig assemblies, which are reassemblies of the reads from sets of overlapping skimmed BAC clones, including mapped WGS reads. The assembly process reworks the contigs (splitting some) and selects them under more stringent criteria than used for the BAC submissions.

The bactig names are of the form "gaab_gnmm" -- the internal names of the two bracketing projects.
