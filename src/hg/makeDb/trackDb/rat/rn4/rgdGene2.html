Description

This track shows protein-coding genes annotated by RGD. Annotations on RGD Genes were downloaded from:

  • ftp://rgd.mcw.edu/pub/data_release/GFF/Corrected_GFF3/.
  • ftp://rgd.mcw.edu/pub/data_release/GENES_RAT.

    • Credits

    Thanks to RGD for providing these annotations.

    References

    Twigger, SN, Shimoyama M, Bromberg S, Kwitek AE, Jacob HJ; RGD Team. The Rat Genome Database, update 2007 – easing the path from disease to data and back again. Nucleic Acids Research, 2007 Jan; 35 (Database issue):D658-62.
