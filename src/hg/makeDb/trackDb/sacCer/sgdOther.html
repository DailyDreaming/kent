This track shows a variety of features in the Saccharomyces cerevisiae genome including tRNA, transposons, centromeres, and ORFs classified as dubious. The data comes from the Saccharomyces Genome Database (SGD). The data was downloaded from ftp://genome-ftp.stanford.edu/pub/yeast/data_download/chromosomal_feature/s_cerevisiae.gff3 on Nov. 27, 2003. Clicking on an item here will bring up a description of the specific item.
