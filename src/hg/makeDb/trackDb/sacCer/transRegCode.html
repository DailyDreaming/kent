Description

This track shows regulatory elements in Saccharomyces cerevisiae supported by experimental and/or computational evidence (Harbison, Gordon, et al., 2004). Harbison, Gordon, et al. performed a genome-wide location analysis with 203 known DNA-binding transcriptional regulators (some under multiple environmental conditions), and identified 11,000 high-confidence interactions between regulators and promoter regions. They then compiled a compendium of motifs for 102 transcriptional regulators based on a combination of their experimental results, cross-species conservation data on four species of yeast, and motifs from the literature, and mapped these motifs to the S. cerevisiae genome.

The details page shows the sequence at a putative binding site compared to the position-specific probability matrix for the identified transcriptional regulator. It also indicates whether the the binding site is supported by experimental (CHIP/CHIP) data and the number of other species of yeast in which the motif is conserved.
