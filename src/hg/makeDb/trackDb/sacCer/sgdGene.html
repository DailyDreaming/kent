This track shows Saccharomyces cerevisiae annotated genes and open reading frames (ORFs) from the Saccharomyces Genome Database (SGD). The data was downloaded from ftp://genome-ftp.stanford.edu/pub/yeast/data_download/chromosomal_feature/s_cerevisiae.gff3 on Nov. 27, 2003. This track excludes the ORFs classified as dubious by SGD. Clicking on an item in this track brings up a display which synthesizes available data on the gene from a wide variety of sources.
