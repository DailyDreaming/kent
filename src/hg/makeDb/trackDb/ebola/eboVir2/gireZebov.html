Description

This track displays variants identified in 101 samples from the Zaire clade of Ebola viruses found by Gire, et al.

Reference

Genomic surveillance elucidates Ebola virus origin and transmission during the 2014 outbreak. Gire SK, Goba A, Andersen G, Sealfon RSG, Park DJ, Kanneh L, Jalloh S, Momoh M, Fullah M, Dudas G et al.. Science 12 September 2014: Vol. 345 no. 6202 pp. 1369-1372 PMID: 25214632
