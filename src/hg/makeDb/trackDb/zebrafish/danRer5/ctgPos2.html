Description

This track shows location of the contigs of the July 2007 $organism Zv7 assembly from The Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute.

A fingerprinted contigs (FPC) map was produced by restriction enzyme digestion with HindIII. Clones were selected from this for high quality sequencing. To create the Zv7 assembly, 1.02 Gb of sequence from 7,823 sequenced clones (7,139 finished and 684 unfinished) produced the scaffold to which WGS assembly contigs were added to fill in the gaps. The chrFinished scaffolds from the Zv3 assembly (danRer1) have been placed on chromosomes since the Zv4 assembly (danRer2). For more information about the assembly, see the assembly track description and the Danio rerio Sequencing Project page at Sanger.

The Zv7 assembly contains 5,036 fragments. The WGS assembly consists of 131,933 contigs having an N50 length of 20,127 bp and 22,961 supercontigs with an N50 length of 1,499,123 bp.(n = 277). Of these, a total of 5010 scaffolds are not mapped to chromosomes: 166 are in Zv7_scaffoldXXXX scaffolds (tied to unplaced FPC contigs), and 166 are in Zv7_NAXX scaffolds (not tied to an FPC contig, chromosome unknown).
