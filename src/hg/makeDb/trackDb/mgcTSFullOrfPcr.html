Description

TWINSCAN predictions proposed for full-ORF PCR confirmation

Method

Multi-exon TWINSCAN predictions that

  1. do not overlap MGC genes or MGC in progress ESTs
  2. do have at least one intron supported by a human EST or mRNA that does not map to any other prediction nearly as well
  3. do not overlap MGC A-list and
  4. are not 'inside' any Genbank human mRNA.
inside means [begin, end] of the alignment of a genbank human mRNA contains the [begin, end] of a Twinscan prediction.
