Description

These tracks display the level of sequence uniqueness of the reference hg18 genome. They were generated using different window sizes and high signal will be found in areas where the sequence is unique.

Methods

The CRG Alignability tracks shows how uniquely k-mer sequences align to a region of the genome. To generate the data, sliding windows of k-mers (where k is 36, 40, 50, 75 or 100 nts) of the human genome sequence were mapped back to the genome using the GEM mapper aligner allowing 0, 1 or 2 mismatches. For each window, a mapability score was computed (S = 1/(number of bases of match found)).
