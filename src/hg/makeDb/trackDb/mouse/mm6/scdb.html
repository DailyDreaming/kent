Description

This track displays aligned clones from the Stem Cell Database (SCDb) to the mouse genome. SCDb is the work of the Lemischka and Moore labs (Princeton), and the Stoeckert lab (Penn). Clones were derived from a subtracted cDNA library from a hematopoietic stem cell (HSC) and an HSC supportive stromal cell line, from both human and mouse cell lines.

Methods

This track was assembled from BLAT alignments of SCDb as of July 15 to the mouse genome (mm6/March. 2005). Default parameters were used for BLAT. For more information about the construction SCDb clone library, see the SCDb home page.
