Description

This track shows Mouse Genome Informatics (MGI) representative transcripts associated with MGI curated alleles. Items are named by a concatenation of the representative transcript ID and allele symbol. All chemical and radiation induced alleles have been combined as "Induced." Floxed/Frt, reporter, knock-out and knock-in targeted alleles are combined as "Targeted." Random-expressed, random-gene disruption, and Cre/Flp transgenes are combined as "Transgenic."

Methods

The MGI database report MGI_PhenotypicAllele.rpt was parsed to provide a list of all curated alleles that have associated representative transcripts.
