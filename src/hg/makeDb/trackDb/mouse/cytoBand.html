Description

The chromosome band track represents the approximate location of bands seen on Giemsa-stained chromosomes under conditions where 400 bands are visible across the entire genome.

Methods

Data are derived from the Mouse400.dat file downloaded from the NCBI ftp site ftp://ftp.ncbi.nih.gov/genomes/M_musculus/maps/mapview/. Band lengths are estimated based on ISCN-defined relative sizes.
