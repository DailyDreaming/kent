Description

This track displays regions that are likely to be useful as microsatellite markers. These are sequences of at least 15 perfect di-nucleotide and tri-nucleotide repeats. These tend to be highly polymorphic in the population.

Methods

This track is a subset of the Simple Repeats track, selecting only repeats of period 2 and 3, with 100% identity and no indels, and with at least 15 copies of the repeat. The Simple Repeats track is created with Tandem Repeats Finder. For more information about the Tandem Repeats Finder, see Benson, G. Tandem repeats finder: a program to analyze DNA sequences. Nucleic Acids Research 27(2), 573-580 (1999).
