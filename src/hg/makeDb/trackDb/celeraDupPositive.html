Description

This region shows similarity > 90% and > 250 bp of repeatmasked sequence to sequences in the Segmental Duplication Database (SDD). For a description of the Whole-genome Shotgun Sequence Detection (WSSD) 'fuguization' method see Bailey, et al (2001) Genome Res 11:1005-17.
