Mirror Sites for the Genome Data

We are trying to distribute the network load of the data to worldwide locations.

Mirror procedures are currently being revised.

List of current Mirror Sites

  • Biochemistry Institute of National Yang-Ming University, Taiwan (with genome sequence, genome browser and BLAT search)
  • Max-Planck-Institut fuer Molekulare Genetik, Germany (with genome sequence)
  • Institut de Genetique Humaine CNRS, France (with genome sequence)
  • Bio-Mirror Project - a world-wide bioinformatics public service, Korea (with genome sequence)
  • Australia/New Zealand aarnet.edu.au HTTP site (with genome sequence, only accessible for .au and .nz)
  • Bioinformatics Unit at International Institute of Molecular and Cell Biology, Poland
