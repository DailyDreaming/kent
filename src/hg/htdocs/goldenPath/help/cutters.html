Description

This track shows restriction enzyme recognition sites. At different zoom levels (number of bases shown in the browser), there are different sets of enzymes shown. The exception is when a custom set of enzymes is chosen to be displayed. In that case, the same restriction sites are shown at all zoom levels.

Zoom Level 
BaseAll of the "primary" enzymes from REBASE.
< 3KbRestriction sites for "primary" enzymes five bases and larger.
≥ 3Kb, < 20KbRestriction sites for "primary" enzymes six bases and larger.
≥ 20Kb, < 250KbJust the following commonly-used enzymes: ClaI, BamHI, BglII, DraI, EcoRI, EcoRV, HindIII, PstI, SalI, SmaI, XmaI.
> 250KbNone displayed.

*"Isoligamer" is a made-up term referring to another enzyme (which isn't an isoscizomer) producing the same sticky ends in the digestion.
