The Variant Annotation Integrator is a research tool for associating annotations from the UCSC database, track hubs and custom tracks with your uploaded set of variant calls.

Initial Release

This is the initial release of the Variant Annotation Integrator. It works in all Internet browsers that we have tested except Internet Explorer 9 and 10. We are still working on the User's Guide. However, we invite you to try out the tool and send us feedback and bug reports.
