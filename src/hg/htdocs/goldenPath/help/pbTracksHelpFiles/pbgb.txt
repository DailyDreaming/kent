The heavy black line in this track shows the portion of the gene that was 
displayed in the window on the last Genome Browser view. If the entire gene
was displayed in the browser, this line will extend for the entire width
of the track image. The label above the leftmost portion of this track shows 
the Genome Browser coordinates corresponding to this view. Click on the 
line to open the UCSC Genome Browser to this position.
