To update an existing source tree, use the command: cvs update -A -d -P. You will want to update your source tree at least once a week. The Genome Browser is under continual development and often has significant enhancements and new features added on a regular basis.

Should you have any comments or questions, contact genome-mirror@cse.ucsc.edu.
