Getting Help with the UCSC Genome Browser

Host a Genome Browser Workshop!     Map

Do you suspect that there is a lot more to the Genome Browser than you have been using? Timeslots are available to host a Genome Browser workshop at your institution. Thanks to the funding support of NHGRI, we are able to offer hands-on Genome Browser training onsite at your institution, tailored to your audience's level of expertise.

For more information or to submit a request to host a workshop, please visit our sign-up page.
