  Jobs with the UCSC Genome Project
 

SOFTWARE QUALITY ASSURANCE ENGINEER - GENOME PROJECT

BASKIN ENGINEERING

Programmer Analyst II

JOB #03-03-28

FULL TIME/CAREER

Variable schedule discussed at interview

FULL SALARY RANGE: $41,900-$75,400/annually

Salary commensurate with qualifications and experience

SUMMARY OF DUTIES: Under the general supervision of the Genome Project Quality Assurance Lead, the selected candidate will be responsible for a portion of the software testing process for the UCSC Genome Browser.

SPECIFIC RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE: create and execute software testing strategies; work with others to direct the testing and bug verification portions of the software release cycle; communicate about technical problems with team members as well as external scientists and researchers; participate in the validation of the data processing pipeline; maintain the issue tracking database; monitor the mailing list.

QUALIFICATIONS INCLUDE (with or without accommodation): BA or BS in Computer Science or equivalent experience; experience working in a UNIX environment; programming experience in at least one of C, Perl or Java; completion of lower division college biology classes or equivalent experience; knowledge of bioinformatics methods and applications or a strong desire to learn; strong communication skills, both written and verbal. Preferred Qualifications: experience working with mySQL or other database systems; experience with web programming; experience testing software in a commercial/professional environment; knowledge, background or experience in molecular biology and/or bioinformatics.

Position is open until filled; initial review of application materials will begin on: 4/21/03.

This position is eligible for full benefits.
