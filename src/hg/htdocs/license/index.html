  Obtaining a Commercial License for the Genome Browser
  A license is required for commercial use of the Genome Browser. No license is needed for academic, nonprofit, and personal use. The data displayed by the browser is freely available for both public and commerical use with a few exceptions. Check the README.txt file in the download directory of a particular assembly to view the use restrictions specific to that release.

To purchase a license:

  • Read and sign the license agreement.
  • Fax a signed copy of the license agreement to Gerald Barnett, Office for IP Management: (831) 459-1658.
  • Follow the instructions in the license agreement for submitting the signed original copy and payment.

After your license application has been processed, you will receive instructions for downloading the browser software and data. CDs are also available upon request.

Licensing inquiries should be made to: genome-www@soe.ucsc.edu.
