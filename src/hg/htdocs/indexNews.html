26 June 2013 - Suggestion Box Released

Due to popular demand, we have created a suggestion box for track, assembly, tool, and other requests. All suggestions will be assigned a reference number so that you may follow up on their progress with our staff. Please limit the use of this form to requests for new features; questions and reports of problems about existing features and data should continue to be directed to our mailing lists. You may reach the suggestion box from the Contact Us page or by clicking here.

12 June 2013 - Ferret Genome Browser Released: A Genome Browser is now available for the Ferret (Mustela putorius furo) assembly released by the Ferret Genome Sequencing Consortium April 2011 (version MusPutFur1.0, UCSC version musFur1). Read more.

22 May 2013 - Gibbon Genome Browser Released: A Genome Browser is now available for the Gibbon (Nomascus leucogenys) assembly released by the Gibbon Genome Sequencing Consortium October 2012 (version Nleu_3.0, UCSC version nomLeu3). Read more.

09 April 2013 - The UCSC Cancer Genomics Browser Has a New Look: The UCSC Cancer Genomics group has recently remodeled the interface of the Cancer Genomics Browser to make it easier to navigate and more intuitive to display, investigate, and analyze cancer genomics data and associated clinical information.
