13 Jan. 2010 - Lifespan of Custom Tracks within Sessions

Due to the popularity of our Custom Tracks and Session tools, we are running out of disk space for saving Custom Tracks within Sessions. Although Sessions themselves are saved for one year, user-generated tracks within Sessions (including Custom Tracks) are saved at the normal rate for all Custom Tracks: 48 hours. However, until now, because we have had space available on the server, we have been attempting to keep Custom Tracks alive for the lifetime of the Session.

Over the next few days, we are going to start actively deleting Custom Tracks within Sessions that have not been accessed within the past 4 months. If you actively use your Custom Tracks within saved Sessions, they will not be deleted.

While we provide short-term Custom Track storage as a service to users who want to view their own data in the UCSC Genome Browser, please keep in mind that we are not a data warehousing service; we do not have the resources and cannot guarantee long-term data storage.

Instead of uploading your data to our servers, please consider using alternate data types such as bigBed, bigWig, or other URL-based tracks.
