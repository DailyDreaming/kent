  About the Working Draft

This site contains a working draft of the human genome, which is over 90% complete. Approximately half of the sequence is in a highly accurate 'finished' state. The other half is merely 'draft' quality. Some care must be taken interpreting draft regions, but these are still often very useful to the working scientist. We encourage you to explore the working draft with the genome browser, which displays the work of many annotators worldwide.



  News

A track representing the Ensembl gene predictions is now available on the April 2001 browser.

The Sept. 2000 and July 2000 versions of the genome are now only available on our archive site. Please see the link in the blue box to the left for more details.

We are in the midst of processing an August 6 freeze of the human genome. We hope to have this up early in September. We ran into some technical difficulties processing the May 30 freeze, and with the sequence already getting somewhat out of date we felt it best just to restart the process with fresher date.
