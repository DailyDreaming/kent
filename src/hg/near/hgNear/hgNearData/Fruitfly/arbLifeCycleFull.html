Description

This column shows the ratio of expression of each gene in a particular phase of the fly life cycle to the expression of the gene in ?? cultures. The experiments were replicated two times each. This track shows just a representative replication.

Black means a gene is neither over nor under expressed in the tissue. Uncolored boxes (white on most browsers) represent missing data. Color-blind users can switch the coloring from red/green to blue/green via the configure button.

Methods

The data in this column were collected as described in the paper Arbeitman MN, et al. (2002), "Gene expression during the life cycle of Drosophila melanogaster", Science 2002 Sep 27;297(5590):2270-5. We retrieved the data from the Stanford Microarray Database.
