Description

This track shows the GNF Expression Atlas ratio of expression of a gene in selected tissues to expression of the gene overall. By default if a gene is more highly expressed in a tissue it will be colored red, and if it is less expressed in the tissue it will be green. This coloring is standard, but the opposite of what a naive user might think. In this case red means go, and green means stop! Color blind users can switch from red/green to blue/green coloring in the configuration page.

Methods

For detailed information about the experiments, see Su et al., "Large-scale analysis of the human and mouse transcriptomes.", PNAS, Mar 19, 2002.
