Description

This shows the Ensembl transcript ID associated with this gene. Ensembl is an automatic gene prediction pipeline and a major genome database and web site run by with the Sanger Institute and The European Bioinformatics Institute. Ensembl is especially effective at capturing genes that are known in one organism to another organism. Compared to other gene predictions they tend to have high specificity but low sensitivity to genes not already associated with characterized mrna or protein sequence.

Clicking here takes you to the Ensembl GeneView for this gene.
