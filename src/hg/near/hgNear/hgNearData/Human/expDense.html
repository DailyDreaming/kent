Description

This column shows the ratio of expression of a gene in a particular tissue to the expression of the gene overall. By default, a gene that is more highly expressed in a tissue is colored red, and a less expressed gene is shown in green. This coloring is standard, but is the opposite of what a naive user might expect: in this case, red means go, and green means stop!

Color-blind users can switch the coloring from red/green to yellow/blue via the configure button.
