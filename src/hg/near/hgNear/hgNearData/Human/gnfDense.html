Description

This track displays selected data from the GNF Expression Atlas on the Affymetrix U95 chip. It shows the ratio of expression of a gene in specific tissues to expression of the gene overall. By default, a gene that is more highly expressed in a tissue is colored red, and a less expressed gene is shown in green. This coloring is standard, but is the opposite of what a naive user might expect: in this case, red means go, and green means stop!

Color-blind users can switch the coloring from red/green to yellow/blue via the configure button.

Methods

For detailed information about these experiments, see Su et al., Large-scale analysis of the human and mouse transcriptomes., PNAS, Mar 19, 2002.
