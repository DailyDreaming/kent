Description

This column shows the ratio of expression of each gene in a particular phase of the worm life cycle to expression of the gene in mixed wild-type N2 cultures. These experiments were replicated several times. This track shows just a representative replication.

By default, a gene that is more highly expressed in a tissue is colored red, and a less expressed gene is shown in green. This coloring is standard, but is the opposite of what a naive user might expect: in this case, red means go, and green means stop!

Color-blind users can switch the coloring from red/green to blue/green via the configure button.

Methods

The data in this column were collected as described in the paper Jiang M, et.al. (2001), "Genome-wide analysis of developmental and sex-regulated gene expression profiles in Caenorhabditis elegans", PNAS 98(1):218-23. We retrieved the data from the Stanford Microarray Database.
